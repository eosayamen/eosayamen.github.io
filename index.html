Enjoy Our Charts

Please click "Enjoy Our Charts". These graphics represent statistics on black students and faculty after 1973. Prior to the 1970's, the university did not collect data on the racial makeup of the campus. Demographic data was not consistently reported. The figures reflect the current make-up of the UMass black population. However, the aberration in the reporting left four (1978-1981) and twelve (1984-1995) years unaccounted for, when documenting the number of black students and their completion rates.
